The worst job at any padel night is being the person everyone asks "skor berapa?" every two minutes. FOM fixes that with one link. Share it once, and everyone watches the live standings from their own phone — no account, no download, nothing to install.

## How it works

When you're running a match, FOM gives you a shareable link. Drop it in the group chat. Anyone who taps it opens a live view of the session: current standings, who's playing on which court, and the scores updating in real time as you enter them.

They don't need FOM Play installed. They don't need to sign in. They just watch.

## Why it's a big deal

- **No more score questions.** Everyone can see the board themselves, including the people sitting out this round.
- **Nobody gets left out.** Friends who couldn't make it can still follow along from home.
- **Zero friction.** The number one reason people don't try a scoring app is "everyone has to download it". With FOM, only the host does.

## Room visibility

If you host regularly, you can also set who finds your games: **public** so anyone can discover them, **friends-only**, or **private** for your closed crew. The share link works the same either way.

Set it up once at the start of the night and you'll never field a "skor berapa?" again. That alone is worth the download.
