You've got the court booked and eight people who said "gas". Now someone has to actually run the night. Good news: with FOM, that someone barely has to do anything. Here's the whole thing, start to finish.

## 1. Create the match

Open FOM Play and tap **Start Match**. Give the night a name — "Friday Night Americano" works fine. This is just so it shows up nicely later in history and your FOM Rewind.

## 2. Pick a format

FOM runs three:

- **Americano** — everyone plays with everyone. The friendliest option, great for mixed groups.
- **Mexicano** — winners face winners, so the games stay close.
- **Match Play** — fixed teams, head to head.

If it's your first time, start with **Americano**. It just works.

## 3. Add your players

Type in names, or pull in friends you've added before. FOM only needs names — nobody else has to download the app or make an account to play or watch.

## 4. Set courts and points

Tell FOM how many courts you've got and how many points each round plays to. Two courts and 24 points per round is a solid default for eight people.

## 5. Generate and play

Tap **Generate Match**. FOM builds the whole schedule — who plays whom, on which court, every round. Score as you go by tapping the numbers. Standings update themselves the moment a match ends.

## That's genuinely it

Share the link so everyone can follow along from their own phone, and when the night's done, generate a FOM Rewind to close it out. You just hosted a padel night and did zero math. Welcome.
