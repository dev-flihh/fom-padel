The match ends, everyone's sweaty and arguing about a line call, and normally that's where the night quietly disappears. **FOM Rewind** is how you keep it. It takes the whole session and turns it into a set of shareable slides — like Spotify Wrapped, but for your padel night.

## What's in a Rewind

FOM builds the slides automatically from the results. You'll get:

- **The Numbers** — total points, games, time on court.
- **The Podium** — your top three.
- **Dream Team** — the pair that clicked best.
- **Match of the Night** — the closest, most dramatic game.
- **The Cupu D'Or** — if Toxic Mode was on, the night's most tragic performance gets its own gold-framed slide.
- **A Photo Dump** — drop in up to ten photos from the night.

## How to make one

1. Finish your match and save the results.
2. Tap the **FOM Rewind** banner on the standings screen.
3. Optionally add photos — they're processed on your phone and never uploaded unless you save the Rewind.
4. Hit generate. A few seconds later, your slides are ready.

## Share it

Swipe through the slides, then download or share straight to your Story. The Cupu D'Or slide, in particular, tends to end up in the group chat within about thirty seconds. Consider that a feature.

That's the loop: play, rank, rewind, repeat. Make one after your next session and see how fast it spreads.
